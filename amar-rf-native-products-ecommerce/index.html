OUR STORY

Welcome to AMAR RF Native Products, where tradition meets innovation in the heart of the Philippines. Our store is a tribute to the timeless craftsmanship of Filipino artisans, blending indigenous techniques with contemporary elegance.

We honor our kagikan, our cultural heritage.

At AMAR RF, we honor our cultural heritage by showcasing exquisite nito and rattan creations. Each piece is meticulously crafted, reflecting the natural beauty and versatility of these traditional materials.

With every purchase, you support local artisans and contribute to the preservation of age-old traditions. Join us on this journey of cultural appreciation and discovery, as we celebrate the enduring legacy of Filipino craftsmanship.
